UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
==================

1. In the Replit file explorer (left sidebar), navigate to the uploads folder
2. Click on either 'pdfs' or 'audio' folder
3. Click the three dots (•••) menu or right-click in the folder
4. Select "Upload file" or drag and drop your files directly into the folder

FOLDER STRUCTURE:
- uploads/pdfs/    - Place your PDF files here
- uploads/audio/   - Place your .wav audio files here

FILE NAMING:
Name your files to match the topic titles for easy mapping, for example:
- gram-positive-cocci.pdf
- gram-positive-cocci.wav

After uploading your files, let me know and I'll integrate them into the app!
